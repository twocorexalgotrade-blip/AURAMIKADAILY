Devkala Atelier

Mumbai · Est. 1948

The House

Three generations.
One handheld craft.

Founded in 1948 by Shri Devkala, this house has shaped temple jewellery for India's most discerning families. Every piece you'll see is hammered, set, and finished by hand inside their second-floor studio off Zaveri Bazaar.

Their signature: a polki technique where each stone is hand-cut, never machine-faceted, then set in the open-back style of Mughal court jewellery.
